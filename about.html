About Us


SmartShopper is the best place to shop smartly!

We help you find the best deals, discounts, and offers to save you time and money. Grocery shopping can be a tedious and expensive task, since the best price is often found by scouting several stores. It requires the shopper to either resort to guesswork or search the websites of several stores for prices, which is not very efficient. Smart Shopper resolves this by simplifying the process into one app.
